ABOUT US

Noble Saprana Co., Ltd. is a Thailand based company exporting products of Thailand to many countries around the world. We start our business by being a partner with Rattana Bauwonkiji Herbal Company, which is the manufacturer of “Chontongmongkon” brand. With more than 30 years wisdom inherited of herbs from parent generation, Chontongmongkon brand has proved that their products have an excellent quality.
We, Noble Saprana Co., Ltd., aimed to distribute Chontongmongkon brand to all over the world, especially Spa and Hotel businesses. Also, we will definitely expand the variety of our products in the future. We are welcome for all inquiries.
