ABOUT US

Noble Saprana Co., Ltd. is a Thailand based company exporting products of Thailand to many countries around the world. We start our business by being a partner with Rattanabovornkijti Herbal Company, which is the manufacturer of famous Thai massage balm, “Chontongmongkon” brand.

Chontongmongkon brand has utilized their knowledges of herbs from the parent generation for more than 30 years to develop an excellent quality massage balm. This is why we, Noble Saprana Co., Ltd., aimed to distribute Chontongmongkon brand to all over the world, especially Spa and Hotel businesses. Also, we will definitely expand the variety of our products in the future.
